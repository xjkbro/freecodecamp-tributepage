Kobe Bryant

The man who inspired a generation of legends.

Here's a timeline of Kobe Bryant's life:


  • 1978 - Kobe Bryant was born Ardmore, Pennsylvannia.
  • 1996 - Straight out of high school, being the youngest player to join the NBA, Bryant was drafted by the Charlotte Hornets where he soon was later traded to the Los Angeles Lakers.
  • 1997 - Bryant as a rookie won the 1997 Slam Dunk Contest.
  • 1998 - Kobe Bryant was the youngest to ever be featured in a All-Star Game.
  • 2000 - Bryant won his first NBA Championship.
  • 2003 - After winning his third NBA Championship, he was titled the youngest to earn a three peat at the age of 23.
  • 2006 - Kobe Bryant scores 81 points against the Toronto Raptors making him the 2nd most points scored in a single game.
  • 2007 - Kobe Bryant had changed his number from 8 to 24 to wear the number he wore in high school.
  • 2009 - Bryant wins his 4th championship.
  • 2010 - Bryant wins his 5th championship.
  • 2013 - Kobe Bryant tore his Achilles tendon.
  • 2016 - In Kobe Bryant's final game against the Jazz, he scores an incredible 60 points. Giving him the title as the oldest person to score over 60 points in a single game.
  • 2019 - Kobe Bryant and his 2nd eldest daughter was in a tragic helicopter accident along with 7 others.
